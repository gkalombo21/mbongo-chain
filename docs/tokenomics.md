# 💰 Mbongo Chain Tokenomics — MBG

## Overview
- **Total Supply:** 10,000,000,000 MBG (fixed)
- **Initial Circulation:** 2,000,000,000 MBG
- **Consensus:** Hybrid Proof of Stake (PoS) + Proof of Useful Work (PoUW)
- **Block Time:** 5 minutes
- **Halving Interval:** Every 5 years
- **Emission Horizon:** ~100 years (final halving by year 100)

The MBG token powers validation, decentralised AI computation, and governance. The design balances early growth incentives with long-term scarcity and sustainability.

---

## Distribution

| Category | Percentage | Notes |
|:--|:--|:--|
| Proof of Stake Validators | 50% | Staking rewards, security incentives |
| Proof of Useful Work GPUs | 30% | AI compute contributions |
| Development Fund | 15% | AI research, infrastructure, grants |
| Mbongo Foundation | 5% | Sustainability, community programs |

Initial circulation (2B MBG) is allocated proportionally to kick-start network security and AI compute, while reserves support long-term growth.

## Halving Mechanism
- The block reward halves every 5 years, limiting inflation.
- By year 100, issuance approaches zero (final halving completed).

```
Year 0-5   : Reward = R
Year 5-10  : Reward = R / 2
Year 10-15 : Reward = R / 4
...
Year 95-100: Reward ≈ R / 2^19
```

This exponential decay favours early network growth while preserving long-term scarcity similar to established halving-based economic models.

---

## Governance
- MBG holders stake tokens to participate in the Mbongo DAO.
- Governance oversees:
  - Protocol upgrades and economic parameters
  - Development fund usage
  - Sustainability initiatives (Mbongo Foundation)
- Voting weight is proportional to staked MBG, encouraging long-term alignment with network success.

---

## Economic Security
- **Security Budget:** PoS rewards ensure validators remain economically incentivised to secure the network.
- **AI Utility:** PoUW rewards create real-world value from GPU compute, anchoring MBG utility in useful work.
- **Treasury Management:** Development fund and foundation allocations provide ongoing financing for R&D, infrastructure, and environmental sustainability.
- **Scarcity:** Halving and the fixed-cap supply reduce inflation gradually, supporting token value as network usage increases.
