# 🌍 Mbongo-Chain Community Guide

## 1. Welcome to the Mbongo Community

Welcome to **Mbongo-Chain** — a global open-source initiative building the future of **AI-powered blockchain infrastructure**.  
We believe in collaboration, transparency, and inclusivity. Every contribution counts, no matter your experience level.

---

## 2. Core Values

- 🤝 **Collaboration** — Work together across borders and disciplines.  
- 🌍 **Inclusivity** — Everyone is welcome, regardless of location, gender, or background.  
- 💡 **Innovation** — Encourage creative and impactful ideas.  
- 🧱 **Transparency** — All decisions are public and open to review.  
- 🔐 **Integrity** — Security, ethics, and honesty are non-negotiable.

---

## 3. Community Structure

| Role | Description |
|------|--------------|
| **Core Team** | Maintains core blockchain and AI modules |
| **Contributors** | Developers, researchers, designers, or writers improving the ecosystem |
| **Validators** | Secure the network via Proof of Stake (PoS) |
| **GPU Operators** | Provide compute power under Proof of Useful Work (PoUW) |
| **Community Moderators** | Enforce rules and manage public channels |
| **DAO Council** | Oversees governance, voting, and protocol upgrades |

---

## 4. Communication Channels

- 💬 **Discord Server** — Real-time discussion (coming soon)  
- 🧠 **Forum** — DAO and proposal discussions (planned 2026)  
- 🧵 **GitHub Discussions** — Technical Q&A and feature requests  
- 📣 **Twitter/X @MbongoChain** — Public announcements and updates  
- 📧 **Email** — contact@mbongo.io  

> 💡 Always keep discussions professional, respectful, and inclusive.

---

## 5. Contributing

- Read the `CONTRIBUTING.md` file before submitting code.  
- All pull requests require code review and automated testing.  
- Issues and bugs should be tagged clearly (`bug`, `enhancement`, `AI`, `blockchain`).  
- Discussions must stay focused on project improvement.  
- Contributors are encouraged to document every function or feature.

---

## 6. Code of Conduct

1. Be kind, respectful, and patient.  
2. Avoid discrimination or harassment of any kind.  
3. No spam, self-promotion, or irrelevant links.  
4. Respect the privacy of all participants.  
5. Report misconduct to moderators or via `security@mbongo.io`.

Violations may result in temporary or permanent suspension.

---

## 7. Community Meetings

- **Monthly calls** (open to all members)  
- **Quarterly town-halls** for roadmap updates  
- **Annual summit** (virtual or in-person)  

Meeting notes are always published on GitHub for transparency.

---

## 8. Recognition and Rewards

- Top contributors may receive **MBG tokens** or NFT badges.  
- Community builders can apply for grants via the **Mbongo DAO Fund**.  
- Academic contributors gain credits in official research publications.

---

## 9. Future Initiatives

- 🌱 **Mbongo Academy** — Train new developers on blockchain + AI fundamentals.  
- 🧩 **Open AI Challenges** — Reward innovation through AI hackathons.  
- 💼 **Partner Network** — Connect institutions, startups, and governments using Mbongo Chain.

---

## 10. Join Us

Whether you’re a developer, researcher, or enthusiast — **you belong here**.  
Let’s build the world’s first ethical, AI-powered blockchain together.
