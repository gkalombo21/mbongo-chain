# 🤖 Mbongo-Chain AI Policy

## 1. Purpose

The **Mbongo-Chain AI Policy** establishes the ethical and technical standards for AI tasks processed through the **Proof of Useful Work (PoUW)** system.  
This ensures that all AI computations contribute positively to society, respect privacy, and align with global ethical norms.

---

## 2. Core Principles

- **Transparency:** All AI workloads must be verifiable and traceable.  
- **Ethical Use:** AI cannot be used for harmful, discriminatory, or illegal activities.  
- **Privacy Preservation:** Data must be anonymized and encrypted before computation.  
- **Sustainability:** PoUW must prioritize energy-efficient GPU workloads.  
- **Accountability:** Every compute job must have a responsible entity (developer, researcher, or organization).

---

## 3. Approved AI Workloads

✅ Allowed tasks:
- Machine learning model training & inference.  
- Data science computations with anonymized data.  
- Computer vision, NLP, and robotics simulation research.  
- Generative AI tasks (text, image, audio) within ethical limits.  
- Scientific, academic, and climate research simulations.

🚫 Prohibited tasks:
- Surveillance or facial recognition for political/military use.  
- Deepfakes or deceptive media generation.  
- Exploitative mining of personal data.  
- AI weaponization, cyber-attacks, or misinformation.  
- Tasks violating international human rights or privacy laws.

---

## 4. Submission & Verification Process

1. **Task Submission:** Developers or partners submit AI tasks through the API or SDK.  
2. **Ethical Screening:** The **AI Governance Council (AIGC)** evaluates each task before approval.  
3. **Execution:** Approved tasks are distributed to GPU nodes for computation under PoUW.  
4. **Result Verification:** Results must include cryptographic proof of execution (ZK-Proof / hash validation).  
5. **Reward Distribution:** Verified results trigger automatic MBG token rewards.

---

## 5. Data Security & Privacy

- All datasets must be **encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256 + TLS)**.  
- No raw personal data may leave the client environment.  
- Differential privacy or federated learning is encouraged for sensitive data.  
- Logs must exclude user-identifiable information.  
- Compute nodes must run in **isolated containers** with sandboxed access.

---

## 6. Ethical Review Board

The **Mbongo Ethical Review Board (MERB)** is a DAO-backed committee that:
- Audits AI task categories quarterly.  
- Reviews flagged or controversial AI workloads.  
- Publishes transparency reports every 6 months.  
- Works jointly with the **AI Governance Council** to define global standards.

---

## 7. Reporting Violations

- Any participant can report unethical or illegal AI use via the **Mbongo DAO portal**.  
- Reports are anonymized and reviewed confidentially.  
- Confirmed violations result in:  
  - Task suspension.  
  - Node disqualification.  
  - MBG staking penalties or slashing.

---

## 8. Enforcement & Updates

- Policy enforced by the **AI Governance Council** via on-chain smart contracts.  
- Updates proposed through DAO voting under the `GOVERNANCE.md` framework.  
- Violations are logged and permanently stored for transparency.

---

## 9. Alignment with Global Frameworks

Mbongo-Chain’s AI ethics align with:
- UNESCO AI Ethics Recommendations.  
- OECD AI Principles.  
- African Union AI Development Charter (2025 Draft).  
- European Union AI Act (2026).

---

## 10. Future Roadmap

- Integrate **AI fingerprinting** to detect harmful model behavior.  
- Introduce **zero-knowledge proofs** for AI verification.  
- Partner with universities and NGOs to promote ethical AI compute use.
