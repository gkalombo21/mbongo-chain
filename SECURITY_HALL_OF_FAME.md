## 💡 How to Get Listed
To be added to this Hall of Fame:

1. Report a verified vulnerability responsibly following our Security Policy.
2. Allow our team to validate and fix the issue.
3. Choose whether to be credited publicly or anonymously.
4. Once validated, your name or alias will appear here within the next update.

---

## 📜 Recognition Rules
- Only confirmed vulnerabilities are eligible for listing.
- Duplicate or already-known reports are not credited.
- Non-security issues (bugs, UX problems, etc.) are not part of this list but may appear in our main `CONTRIBUTORS.md` file.
