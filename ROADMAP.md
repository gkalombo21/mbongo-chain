# 🗺️ Mbongo-Chain Roadmap

## 1. Vision

Mbongo-Chain is building a **hybrid blockchain** combining:
- **Proof of Stake (PoS)** for governance and validation.  
- **Proof of Useful Work (PoUW)** for distributed AI and GPU computing.  

Our mission is to **democratize access to AI infrastructure** by rewarding users who contribute real computational power.  

The ultimate goal is to make Mbongo-Chain a **global network for AI computation**, accessible from anywhere — Africa, North America, Europe, and beyond.

---

## 2. Guiding Principles

- 🌍 **Global inclusion:** Empower underrepresented regions with AI compute access.  
- ⚙️ **Interoperability:** Seamless integration with Cosmos SDK, Ethereum, and Web3 standards.  
- 🤝 **Open collaboration:** 100% open-source and community-governed.  
- 🧠 **AI-driven innovation:** Smart task allocation and decentralized model training.

---

## 3. Development Phases

### **Phase 1 — Foundation (Q4 2025)**
- ✅ Setup repository and project structure  
- ✅ Initialize Go backend and node skeleton  
- ✅ Define governance, security, and AI policy  
- 🧩 Implement core blockchain logic (PoS + PoUW prototype)  
- 🧱 Establish developer onboarding and contribution rules  

**Goal:** Fully operational testnet for PoS + GPU simulation

---

### **Phase 2 — Testnet Expansion (Q1–Q3 2026)**
- 🌐 Launch “**Mbongo Testnet Alpha**”  
- 🧠 Integrate AI Compute Marketplace (PoUW live tests)  
- ⚙️ Add user wallet + staking dashboard  
- 🔗 Introduce REST and GraphQL APIs  
- 💸 Implement MBG tokenomics model  

**Goal:** First decentralized AI jobs successfully executed on testnet.

---

### **Phase 3 — Mainnet Launch (Q4 2026)**
- 🚀 Deploy Mbongo-Chain mainnet  
- 💡 Enable full PoUW validation with live rewards  
- 🧰 Launch node management dashboard  
- 🔒 Security audit and smart contract certification  
- 🤝 Launch DAO and on-chain governance  

**Goal:** Stable, scalable mainnet serving AI + blockchain use cases globally.

---

### **Phase 4 — Global Adoption (2027–2028)**
- 🌍 Partnerships with African and global data centers  
- 🧩 SDK and API release for third-party AI integration  
- 💼 Enterprise GPU-as-a-Service (GaaS) program  
- 🧬 Research collaboration with universities  
- 📊 Publish Mbongo Research Reports (AI + Blockchain metrics)

**Goal:** Mbongo becomes a top-tier decentralized AI infrastructure network.

---

### **Phase 5 — AI Autonomy (2029+)**
- 🤖 AI-assisted governance (DAO automation)  
- 🧠 Decentralized AI models (on-chain inference)  
- 💠 Multi-chain bridge with other AI-focused networks  
- 🔋 Sustainable energy & green GPU mining program  

**Goal:** Self-sustaining AI-blockchain ecosystem powering global innovation.
